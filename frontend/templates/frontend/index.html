Who are We?

We are the Knights of the Tiber, the Latin club at St. Ed's and the only Latin club in the state of Ohio with a name as cool as ours. All students enrolled in Latin are eligible to join the club and we meet every community period E.

What do you do?

Contrary to popular belief, Latin is alive and well at St. Ed's. In addition to the fun and antics that take place during our meetings, we also travel to the state and sometimes even the national Latin convention! Knights compete in a variety of different academic contests ranging from tests, to Certamen (a Latin academic challenge), to Latin oratory and recitation.
